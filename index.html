About Me

I am originally from El Paso TX and moved to Denver, CO about seven years ago. I worked for about 3.5 years in the television industry as a graphics artist and production assistant. After moving to Denver CO I entered the financial services industry working for Transamerica. I have spent the last 6.5 years there with the last three being in management. I am currently going back to school at Regis University pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science. In addition to this I am enrolled in a coding bootcamp hosted at Denver University specializing in web development.

Last year I completed a team based race called the Wild West Relay. We ran from Fort Collins CO to Steamboat Springs CO. I ran about 23.5 miles total over the course of 36 hours. It was definitely one of the greatest experiences of my life; and once I finish school and the bootcamp program I will be back to pursuing fitness & running in my free time.
